Understanding Genomic Data Analysis

Published on March 18, 2024 | By Nisha Paudel

Genomic data analysis is a powerful approach to understanding the blueprint of life. With advances in sequencing technologies, bioinformatics tools are essential for processing and interpreting large-scale biological data.

The Role of Bioinformatics

Bioinformatics bridges biology and computational science. It involves using software tools to analyze genetic sequences, identify mutations, and predict protein structures.
