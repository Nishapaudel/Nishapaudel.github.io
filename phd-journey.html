Falling in Love with My PhD Project

Published on March 18, 2024 | By Nisha Paudel

My journey into academia started in the breathtaking mountains of Nepal. Growing up surrounded by nature's beauty, I developed a deep curiosity about the world and a passion for scientific research.

From Nepal to Australia

Moving to Australia for my PhD was both exciting and challenging. The transition from the serene landscapes of Nepal to the bustling research environment of an international university was a transformative experience.

"Every challenge I faced only deepened my love for my research." – Nisha Paudel

The Beauty of Research

Research is not just about finding answers; it’s about asking the right questions. Through my work, I’ve had the chance to contribute to scientific discoveries while growing as an individual.

Lessons Learned

  • Resilience is key in academic research.
  • Collaboration enhances scientific discovery.
  • Passion fuels perseverance.
  • Every setback is a lesson in disguise.

My PhD journey has been an adventure filled with learning, challenges, and personal growth. Through this experience, I have truly fallen in love with the process of discovery.
